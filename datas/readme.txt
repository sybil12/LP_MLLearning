datasets:

1 mouth
contains 500 neutral images and 500 smile images of lips, for classification and segmentation tasks.

2 eye
contains 500 closeeye images and 500 openeye images for train, 50 closeeye images and 50 openeye images for val,for classification and segmentation tasks.

3 head
contains 500 lefthead images and 500 righthead images for train, 50 lefthead images and 50 righthead images for val,for classification and keypoints detection tasks.
